DELIVERING MULTIPLE HOUSING PROJECTS FOR OUR COMMUNITY IS THE MOST IMPORTANT THING TO US

The heart of any community is its members. Parklands Property Group was set up to allow investors to firstly come together and share good and bad investment experiences so we can all learn and make better decisions moving forward. Secondly it was set up to provide strategies for our members to invest into safely by acting as a developer rather than just a house buyer. By doing so unlocks the property market opportunities and allows our members to participate in developer profit which is usually reserved for large companies.

OUR HISTORY

Parklands Properties Group is the official name of our investment community established in 2022 however the community has been investing into housing developments since 2016. The aim of the community was to make investing in property affordable for everyone and provide access to worldwide markets without any of the hassle.
