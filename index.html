Why Choose us

Parklands Properties Group is the official name of our investment community established in 2022 however the community has been investing into housing developments since 2016. The aim of the community was to make investing in property affordable for everyone and provide access to worldwide markets without any of the hassle.

We have offices in London, Malaysia and Dubai

Our vision is to continue to grow our community and help more first time buyers get on the property ladder and experienced buyers a hassle free way to build, rent or sell property in the highest demand locations across the globe.

Invest together, build together

Parklands Property Group believe that together we can grow faster, safer and stronger. Together we get access to investment opportunities that individually would never be offered. Our larger buying power provides discounts on everything we invest in.

Strong and experienced leadership team

Our team has a combined experience of 40 years investing in property investing which ensures our projects are fully vetted before being offered to the group.

Huge community of investors

We have a very large community of investors who have been investing with us for many years. Keeping our community happy and feeding them with high yielding investments is our #1 priority.
