Overview

Parklands @ Liverpool is a 198 unit housing development consisting of 2 bedroom terrace, 3 bedroom semi-detached and 4 bedroom detached landed properties. Land area ranges from 100-300 sq meters with front and back gardens, parking and driveways.

Parklands @ Liverpool is located in the Suburb of Melling just 10km from Liverpool City centre in the UK adjacent to existing properties and a brand new completed housing project called Rainbow fields from developer Bellway Homes.
